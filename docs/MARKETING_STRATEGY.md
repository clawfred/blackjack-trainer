## 1. Platform Strategy

### Primary: TikTok + Instagram Reels

**Why these two:**
- Casino/gambling education content is *exploding* on TikTok — short-form "what would you do?" format gets massive engagement
- TikTok's algorithm favors new accounts with strong content (you don't need followers to go viral)
- Instagram Reels cross-pollinates with the carousel format Kerem wants
- Both skew 21-40 demographic — perfect for casual casino-goers
- Educational gambling content is compliant on both platforms as long as you don't promote specific casinos or encourage deposits

**Why NOT other platforms (yet):**
- YouTube Shorts — add in Month 2 (repurpose TikTok content)
- Twitter/X — low priority, casino twitter is mostly degen/crypto overlap
- Reddit — r/blackjack is good for community seeding but not primary growth channel

### Posting Cadence

| Platform | Posts/Day | Format |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| TikTok | 3-4 | Short video (15-60s) |
| Instagram | 2 Reels + 1 Carousel | Mixed |
| Instagram Stories | 2-3 | Polls, quizzes, behind-the-scenes |

**Total daily output: ~6-8 pieces** (with heavy cross-posting/repurposing)

### Account Identity

- **Handle:** `@blackjacktrainer` (or `@bjtrainer` / `@learnblackjack` if taken)
- **Bio:** "Learn perfect blackjack strategy 🃏 Free app in bio 👇"
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, dark theme (deep green felt + white/gold accents). NOT neon Vegas kitsch.
- **Tone:** Smart friend who knows the game, not a guru. Casual but authoritative.

## 2. Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: "What Would You Do?" (Core — 40% of content)

Kerem's carousel/video concept. This is the engine.

**Format A — Carousel (Instagram):**
- Slide 1: Hand visual — dealer upcard + player cards. "What's the play?"
- Slide 2: "Basic strategy says: DOUBLE ✅"
- Slide 3: Why — EV explanation in plain English
- Caption: Engaging hook + "Download the app to practice 👇"

**Format B — Video (TikTok/Reels):**
- 0-3s: Show the hand. "What do you do here?"
- 3-5s: Pause (let them think / comment)
- 5-15s: Reveal + explanation
- Trending audio underneath

**Why this works:** Interactive content gets 2-5x more comments. People LOVE arguing about hands. Wrong answers in comments = algorithm fuel.

**Content bank:** There are ~270 unique hand combinations in basic strategy. At 3-4 hands/day, that's 2-3 months of content before repeating. Prioritize the hands people get wrong most:
- Soft 17 vs dealer 6 (double, not hit)
- 12 vs dealer 2 (hit, not stand)
- Pair of 8s vs dealer 10 (split, always)
- 16 vs dealer 10 (hit, surrender if allowed)
- 11 vs dealer Ace (double in most games)

### Pillar 2: "Casino Mistakes" (Myth-busting — 25%)

**Formats:**
- "3 things blackjack dealers wish you knew"
- "Stop doing THIS at the blackjack table"
- "The #1 mistake that costs you money"
- "Insurance is a SUCKER bet — here's why"
- "Why you should NEVER play 6:5 blackjack"

**Why this works:** Negative/corrective hooks outperform positive ones. "You're doing it wrong" stops the scroll.

### Pillar 3: "Did You Know?" (Education — 20%)

- House edge breakdowns (6:5 vs 3:2, single vs multi-deck)
- History of card counting
- How casinos actually make money on blackjack
- EV explanations made simple
- "What basic strategy actually is" (most people don't know)

**Why this works:** Positions the brand as the authority. Builds trust. These are shareable/saveable.

### Pillar 4: App & Lifestyle (Promotion — 15%)

- App UI walkthroughs / feature reveals
- "Practice session" screen recordings
- User testimonials / before-after stories
- Vegas trip prep content ("learn before you go")
- Memes about casino experiences

**Why this works:** Converts followers to downloaders. But keep it under 15% — nobody follows an ad account.

## 3. Launch Plan & Content Calendar

### Phase 0: Account Warm-Up (Weeks 1-2)

**Goal:** Establish accounts, signal to algorithms, avoid shadowban.

**Week 1:**
- Create accounts with final branding (profile pic, bio, link)
- Follow 50-100 accounts in the space (casino creators, poker players, gambling educators)
- Like/comment on 20-30 posts daily (genuine engagement, not spam)
- Post 1x/day — simple, low-production "What would you do?" hands
- NO link in bio yet — just build presence

**Week 2:**
- Increase to 2x/day posting
- Start engaging with commenters on your own posts
- Add the App Store link to bio
- Post first carousel on Instagram
- Experiment with 2-3 trending sounds on TikTok

### Phase 1: Content Engine (Weeks 3-6)

**Goal:** Full posting cadence. Find what resonates.

- Ramp to full 3-4 posts/day on TikTok, 3/day on Instagram
- A/B test: video vs carousel, different hook styles, with/without face
- Batch-produce content: create 1 week of content in 2-3 hours
- Start tracking which hands/topics get most engagement
- Begin building a swipe file of top-performing hooks from competitors

## 4. Growth Tactics

### Hashtags

**Primary (use on every post):**
- `#blackjack` `#blackjackstrategy` `#blackjacktips`
- `#casino` `#casinotips` `#gambling`
- `#learnblackjack` `#basicstrategy`

**Secondary (rotate):**
- `#vegas` `#lasvegas` `#vegastrip` `#casinolife`
- `#tablegames` `#21` `#cardgames`
- `#gamblingtips` `#casinogames` `#blackjackplayer`

**Growth/Discovery:**
- `#didyouknow` `#themoreyouknow` `#learntok`
- `#casinotok` `#gamblingtok` `#blackjacktok`

**TikTok:** 3-5 hashtags per post. Instagram: 15-20 in first comment.

### Engagement Hacking

1. **Ask questions in every caption** — "Would you hit or stand? Comment 👇"
2. **Reply to every comment in the first hour** — signals engagement to algorithm
3. **Pin controversial/wrong answers** — drives debate threads
4. **"Part 2?" bait** — end videos with "Should I explain card counting next?"
5. **Duet/Stitch** other casino creators' content with your strategy take

### Collaborations

**Micro-influencer targets (1K-50K followers):**
- Casino vloggers who post trip content
- Poker educators branching into table games
- "Vegas tips" lifestyle accounts
- Finance/math creators (EV/probability angle)

**Collaboration formats:**
- "Test them on basic strategy" — quiz a casino influencer
- Guest explanations on their channel
- "React to their blackjack hands"

**Outreach template:**
> Hey [name]! Love your casino content. I built a blackjack training app and would love to collab — I could quiz you on basic strategy for a fun video. Interested?

### Paid vs Organic Split

| Phase | Organic | Paid | Monthly Budget |
|-------|---------|------|----------------|
| Weeks 1-6 | 100% | 0% | $0 |
| Weeks 7-12 | 80% | 20% | $150-300 |
| Post-launch | 60% | 40% | $500-1000 |

**Paid strategy:**
- Only boost posts that already performed well organically (>5% engagement rate)
- Target: Males 21-45, interests: casino, blackjack, poker, Las Vegas, gambling
- Objective: App installs (once live) or profile visits (pre-launch)
- Start with $5/day per creative, scale winners to $15-20/day

### TikTok-Specific Compliance

TikTok restricts gambling promotion. Stay safe:
- ✅ Educational content about strategy/math
- ✅ "Learn before you play" framing
- ❌ Never say "guaranteed wins" or "make money gambling"
- ❌ Never promote specific casinos or betting platforms
- ❌ Never show real money being wagered
- Frame everything as EDUCATION and ENTERTAINMENT, not gambling promotion

## 5. Metrics & KPIs

### North Star Metric
**App downloads from social media** (tracked via UTM links + App Store attribution)

### Weekly Dashboard

| Metric | Week 1-2 Target | Month 1 Target | Month 3 Target |
|--------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| TikTok followers | 100 | 1,000 | 10,000 |
| IG followers | 50 | 500 | 5,000 |
| Avg views/video (TT) | 200 | 1,000 | 5,000+ |
| Engagement rate | 5%+ | 5%+ | 4%+ |
| App downloads/week | — | 50 | 500 |
| Best single video views | 1,000 | 10,000 | 100,000 |

### Key Engagement Metrics
- **Save rate** (most important on IG — signals value)
- **Comment count** (WWYD posts should average 10+ comments)
- **Share rate** (people sharing hands to friends = organic growth)
- **Profile visits → link clicks** (funnel conversion)

### What "Working" Looks Like
- 1 in 20 TikToks breaks 10K views within first month
- WWYD posts consistently get 2x the comments of other formats
- Profile-to-download conversion rate >2%
- Organic growth >20% week-over-week in months 1-2

### What "Not Working" Looks Like (Pivot Triggers)
- <500 avg views after 30 days of consistent posting → reassess content quality/hooks
- <2% engagement rate → hooks aren't stopping the scroll
- Followers growing but zero downloads → CTA or funnel is broken
- One format massively outperforms → shift ratio (don't stay balanced for balance's sake)

## 6. Daily Execution Plan

### Daily Routine (~90 minutes/day)

**Morning Block (30 min) — Create:**
1. Pick 3-4 hands from the [content bank](#hand-queue) for today
2. Create visuals (screenshot from app or Canva template)
3. Write captions using hook formulas (see below)
4. Schedule posts via Later or TikTok drafts

**Midday Block (20 min) — Engage:**
1. Reply to ALL comments from last 12 hours
2. Like/comment on 10 posts in the niche
3. Check DMs

**Evening Block (20 min) — Post & Engage:**
1. Post evening content (TikTok algorithm likes 6-9 PM posts)
2. Reply to new comments
3. Engage with 10 more niche accounts

**Weekly Block (60 min, Sunday) — Review & Plan:**
1. Check analytics: what performed, what flopped
2. Plan next week's content calendar
3. Batch-create 5-7 carousels in Canva
4. Update the hand queue (mark used hands, add new angles)

### Hook Formulas (Rotate These)

1. **"What would you do?"** — "You have 16, dealer shows 10. What's the play?"
2. **"Stop doing this"** — "If you hit on soft 18 vs 6, you're burning money"
3. **"Most people get this wrong"** — "90% of players misplay this hand"
4. **"The math says"** — "Here's why doubling 11 vs Ace is correct"
5. **"Dealers hate this"** — "The one move that actually gives you an edge"
6. **"Vegas secret"** — "What the casino doesn't want you to know about 6:5 tables"
7. **"$X mistake"** — "This common mistake costs the average player $200/trip"

### Content Production Shortcuts

- **Batch creation:** Spend 2 hours Sunday making 20+ pieces for the week
- **Template everything:** One Canva carousel template, swap the cards each time
- **Cross-post aggressively:** Every TikTok → IG Reel (remove watermark with SnapTik). Every IG carousel concept → TikTok video version
- **Use the app itself:** Screen-record the Blackjack Trainer app for half your content. It IS the content.
- **Trending audio:** Check TikTok's Creative Center daily for trending sounds. Slap them on WWYD reveals.

## Appendix: Competitive Landscape

### Who's Already Doing This

- **Casino influencers** (NGSlot, VegasLowRoller) — focus on slots, not strategy. Blackjack education is underserved.
- **Poker educators** on TikTok are huge (Daniel Negreanu, Rampage Poker) — proves the "strategy education" format works on social. Blackjack doesn't have an equivalent yet.
- **Blackjack-specific accounts** are mostly low-quality or card-counting focused. Clean, "learn basic strategy" content is a gap.

### Our Advantage

1. **We have the app** — every piece of content has a natural CTA
2. **Basic strategy is finite and provable** — unlike poker, there are mathematically correct answers. Perfect for "reveal" content.
3. **The audience is massive** — blackjack is the most popular table game in the world. Most players have never studied basic strategy.
4. **Education framing** avoids platform compliance issues that gambling content faces
